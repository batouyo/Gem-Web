Macao Polytechnic University student team wins Jingdong Health AI Global Healthcare Competition

Oct  3  2024 , Thu

On the international stage of Jingdong Health AI Global Healthcare Competition, Macao Polytechnic University's student teams emerged from the crowd with impressive results thanks to their outstanding technical strength, forward-looking innovative thinking and close teamwork. Among them, the master's student team won the Winner's Prize and the doctoral student team won the Third Prize.

The master's student team “Kang Yang Hui” is committed to the digital transformation of healthcare by utilizing advanced big language modeling technology. It provides intelligent and personalized health management services to improve patient experience and optimize the medical process, demonstrating the deep integration of medical treatment and health management. The doctoral team “Innovative Application of General Ultrasound Artificial Intelligence Large Models” focuses on the development of general ultrasound artificial intelligence large models to provide solutions for disease prediction and tissue segmentation. The model has high versatility and innovative design, which is expected to promote the development of medical diagnosis and provide strong support for future medical diagnosis, reflecting the cutting-edge application of artificial intelligence in medical image processing.

Students from Macao Polytechnic University have achieved excellent results in the Jingdong Health AI Global Healthcare Competition, demonstrating the university's profound strength in the application of big data and artificial intelligence technologies. This achievement not only affirms the university's educational achievements, but also provides a solid foundation for future academic research and technological innovation. The school will continue to support students' participation in international events to promote academic exchanges and technological progress.
