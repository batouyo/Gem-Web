HuiLanFengYun Project Wins Bronze Award at the 14th "Challenge Cup" National Finals

Mar  03  2025 , Mon

At the National Finals of the Main Competition of the 14th "Challenge Cup" Qinchuangyuan Chinese University Students' Entrepreneurial Plan Contest, the project titled HuiLanFengYun – Leader in China's Information-Based Smart Tourism Service Industry, jointly participated by 11 students including Chenglin Yang, Shujing Jiang, Junhua Xian, Pusuo Feng, Zhijian Wei, Weiqi Ma, Luyao Liu, Sa Li, Manjia Li, Nengsheng Wei, and Wei Pang, won the Bronze Award.

The project, with its unique innovative concept and promising market prospects, garnered high praise from the judges. Additionally, the project's advisors, Yue Sun, Tao Tan, Chan-Tong Lam, Junjie Wu, and Bote Zhang, were also awarded honorary certificates in recognition of their outstanding contributions during the project's guidance process.

This award is not only an affirmation of the project team's innovative capabilities and practical achievements but also serves as encouragement for them to continue their dedication to the development of China's information-based smart tourism service industry. We eagerly anticipate that the HuiLanFengYun project will achieve even more remarkable accomplishments in the future.
